About Flutterwave

Description

Flutterwave is a trusted brand that unlocks boundless payment opportunities for enterprises, individuals, small businesses, emerging markets, and startups. With a wide range of products and services, Flutterwave offers endless possibilities for businesses of all sizes. For enterprises, Flutterwave provides enterprise payment solutions that help grow their business.


They can easily accept online payments from around the world in different currencies and experience effortless cross-border payouts and transfers. For physical businesses, Flutterwave offers point-of-sale (POS) systems with multiple payment methods to enhance their operations. For individuals, Flutterwave offers seamless products for smooth transactions and efficient money management.


They can conveniently send money to loved ones residing overseas, shop online from their favorite businesses, and find and buy event tickets. Developers will also find Flutterwave's well-documented and easy-to-use APIs beneficial, allowing them to integrate multiple payment functionalities into their applications. With Flutterwave, the possibilities are endless.


Whether you're an enterprise, a small business, or an individual, Flutterwave provides the tools and services you need to succeed in the global marketplace. Start accepting payments, selling products online, and building financial tools with Flutterwave today
